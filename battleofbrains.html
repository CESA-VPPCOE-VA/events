Winner Of Battle Of Brains


Event Name:Battle Of Brains

04/08/2023 Ekta Champaneri

314 classroom
Total number of participants : 47
Faculty Coordinator : Prof. Shefali Raina

Outcome of the event:

Students will be able to do c programming language related programs. The technical event “ Battle of Brains ” was organized by CESA (Computer Engineering Students Association) under the umbrella in association with CSI (Computer Society of India) on August 04th, 2023. Students across the department participated in the event.

"Battle of Brains" event was successfully held in two rounds. The first round included a Quiz test. Rules and regulations of the quiz were explained at the beginning. The Quiz round was the eliminator round, and in that round, all the participants participated; out of them, the top 20 participants were selected for round 2.

In Round 2, the 20 participants were divided into 4 groups. After that, the rules and regulations of that round were explained by the head of the technical team of CESA. Round 2 was about the "Rapid Fire" round. In this round, four teams had to answer sets of questions based on the C programming language. In each set of questions, there were 5 questions 3 were theory-based and 2 were coding-based. Teams had to answer them within the given time.

The winners of the "Battle Of Brains" event are as follows:

Winner Team :1.Nihaar Mascarenhas 2.Karthikey Singh 3.Siddhesh Howale 4.Omkar Ardekar 5.Smita Ranjhane Heartily Congratulations to the winning team.
