BGMI Tournament


Event Name: BGMI Tournament

09/02/2024 Ekta Champaneri

Venue: Computer Labs 3rd Floor
Total number of teams : 33 teams
Faculty Coordinator : Prof. Shefali Raina

Objective of the event:

The primary goal of this event is to showcase top BGMI talent and promote mobile Esports.

Outcome of the event:

Students will be able to recognize BGMI talent and strengthen community bonds.

The event “ BGMI tournament ” was organized by CESA (Computer Engineering Students Association) on September 14th, 2023. Students across the department participated in the event. Total 33 teams participated in this tournament.

The BGMI Tournament showcased the incredible talent and competitiveness within the BGMI esports community. It highlighted the growth of mobile esports and its potential to rival traditional gaming platforms. The event brought players and fans together in celebration of their passion for BGMI.

"BGMI tournament" event was successfully held in two rounds.Before the competition starts rules and regulations were explained by the technical team of CESA.In Round 1 total 33 teams were divided into two groups,among them competitions were held and from each group 8 teams were selected.Live streaming of this tournament was held.

In Round 2, the 8 teams of each group were selected.On basis of their points in classic mode BGMI Tournament 3 Winning teams were declared and awarded with the cash prizes.

After successfully completing the event, we found our winner.

The winners of the "BGMI Tournament" event are as follows:

1. BKM 2. ARCANE 3. TYRANT Heartily Congratulations to the winning teams.

Overall, "BGMI Tournament " proved to be a thrilling and intellectually stimulating event, providing participants with valuable opportunities to their programming skills, tackle challenging problems, and foster teamwork and collaboration. We extend our congratulations to all the participants and winners for their outstanding performances and commendable efforts throughout the event.
