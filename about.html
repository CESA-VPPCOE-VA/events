Fun Facts

Our Beloved HOD Sir

From Kolhapur to Mumbai , from Assistant Professor to Head Of Department (HOD) Dr Rais Mulla. He is a gem to behold! After his Graduation in Information Technology He pursued M.tech and started his Career in Vppcoe in the year 2015 as Assistant Professor and since then he has been guiding light to the students of Vppcoe. His dedication and hardwork towards his students & institute got him the position of HoD Of Computer Engineering Department in August 2022 after completing his PhD . Goes by the belief that Innovation and Improvement goes hand in hand! Blessing his students not only with academic knowledge but also with life lessons. His multitasking skills aims at completing the vision of the institute.
